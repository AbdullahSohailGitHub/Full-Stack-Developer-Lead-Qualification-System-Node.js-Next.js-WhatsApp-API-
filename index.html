The Problem

Good leads go cold.
Your BDC team doesn't have time to sort them.

Without an automated scoring system, every lead — whether they're ready to buy or completely unqualified — gets the same follow-up effort. That means wasted time, missed buyers, and revenue left on the table.

📉

No Lead Prioritization

Filter A buyers (ready to close) compete with unqualified leads for your BDC team's attention. The best prospects wait while the team chases dead ends.

Every qualification question answered manually means delays of hours — or days. By then, the buyer has already gone to a competitor.
